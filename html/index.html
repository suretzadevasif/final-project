Our Story

Pion fashion center is located in Port Baku. Pion is a project of 2 friends and it is becoming successful. This project was our childhood dream. After reaching a certain age, we decided to make this dream come true. At first, we started with a small budget and it was not successful from the day we started. There were many stores like us operating in the market and their budgets were big. It's true that sometimes we got discouraged and wanted to stop the project. Then, over time, everything happened as we wanted and our result is in sight.
